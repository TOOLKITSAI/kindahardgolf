üéÆ The Birth of Kinda Hard Golf

"It's fun! But kinda hard" - The perfect description that gave the game its name

Kinda Hard Golf is a daily physics golf puzzle game created by James O'Halloran, the creative force behind Mighty Pebble Games. James describes it as one of those ideas that "glued me to the keyboard for a few weeks" - a passion project that captured his complete attention.

Built with PixiJS, the game emerged when early testers consistently reacted with "It's fun! But kinda hard" - and thus, the perfect name was born. The game presents a new challenge every day at midnight UTC - miss one and it's gone forever! This "one level per day" format, inspired by Wordle, creates anticipation, builds daily habits, and keeps the entire community synchronized.
