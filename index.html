üöÄ Quick Start

  1. Drag the ball to aim - the longer you drag, the more power
  2. Release to shoot - watch the exaggerated physics take over!
  3. Hit checkpoint flags to save progress (or try Kinda Infuriating mode without them!)
  4. Reach the hole at the top in as few strokes as possible

Remember: Every missed shot teaches you about physics, angles, and power - that's "productive failure" in action!

üéÆ Controls

Mouse/Touch

  • Click/Tap & Hold: Grab the ball
  • Drag Backwards: Aim opposite to launch direction
  • Release: Launch upward
  • F5/Refresh: Restart level

üí° Pro Tips

1. Master the Power

Don't always use full power! Sometimes a gentle tap is all you need. Practice controlling your shot strength.

2. Use the Walls

Bouncing off walls can help you reach tricky spots. Think like a pool player!

3. Plan Your Route

Before shooting, visualize your climbing path. Look for platforms and ledges to reach the top.

4. Prioritize Checkpoint Flags

Land on checkpoint flags early! If you fall, you'll respawn at the last flag instead of the bottom.

5. Learn from Mistakes

Each failed shot teaches you about the physics. Pay attention to how the ball moves!

6. Don't Miss a Day

Like Wordle, there's one new level daily. Miss it and it's gone forever - play every day!

üß† The Kinda Hard Philosophy

Created by James O'Halloran (a self-proclaimed terrible golfer), Kinda Hard Golf embraces "productive failure" - where every missed shot teaches you something new.

  • Perfect Difficulty: Hard enough to engage, achievable enough to satisfy
  • Daily Scarcity: Like Wordle, miss today's level and it's gone forever
  • Community Sync: Everyone plays the same level - compare and compete!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

When does the daily level reset?

New levels appear every day at midnight UTC (7 PM EST / 4 PM PST).

Can I replay old levels?

No, once a daily level is gone, it's gone forever - just like Wordle! Don't miss your chance.

How is par determined?

Par is set by James O'Halloran based on the optimal climbing path to the top.

Do I need to create an account?

No! The game is 100% free with no registration required. Scores are stored locally.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes! The game works perfectly on any device with a modern web browser.

How do I share my score?

Click the "Share Score" button after completing a level to share on social media.
