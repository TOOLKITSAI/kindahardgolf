Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Kinda Hard Golf is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Data: Your scores and game progress are stored locally in your browser
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how players interact with our game (page views, session duration, device type)
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Understand game usage patterns to create better daily challenges

3. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your scores or progress
  • Clearing your browser data will reset your game progress

4. Cookies

We use cookies only for:

  • Google Analytics to track anonymous usage statistics
  • Remembering your game preferences (if any)

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics
  • Cloudflare: For website hosting and performance

6. Children's Privacy

Kinda Hard Golf is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect personal information, there is minimal security risk. We use HTTPS encryption for all communications with our servers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local game data at any time through your browser settings
  • Disable cookies in your browser
  • Play the game without any data collection by using incognito/private browsing mode

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
